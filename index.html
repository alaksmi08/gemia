Gem Island Resort & Spa is your ultimate private island getaway with only one resort. It is one of the few privately owned islands in Malaysia built with its superb natural beauty still intact. Gem Island is located in a Marine Conservation Park with crystal clear waters, a spectacular underwater seascape of corals and marine life as some of its natural wonders. Built along the ridges of the coastline and maintaining its lush greenery, it offers facilities such as an in-house Spa for you to enjoy a luxurious holiday and rejuvenate all your senses.
